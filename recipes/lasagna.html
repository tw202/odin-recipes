Lasagna

Description

This is the best recipe there ever was. It is the most delicious thing you will have ever eaten in your entire life :)

Ingredients

  • 3 large zuchini, finely grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Batter

  • 1 egg
  • 5 tablespoons all-purpose flouw
  • 4 tablespoons parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • oil for frying

Toppings

  • 1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
