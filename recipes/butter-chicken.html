Butter Chicken

Description

My favourite butter chicken recipe (and the only one i know).

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast

For the marinade

  • tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tbsp. lime juice or lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup yogurt (150 g)
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 clove/cloves garlic
  • 1 piece(s) ginger, the size of a thumb

Additionally

  • 4 tablespoon butter
  • 1 onion(s)
  • 500 g tomatoes, strained
  • v1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • 1 piece(s) ginger, thumb-sized
  • 1 clove/garlic
  • 1 tbsp. honey
  • 150 ml cream
  • some coriander leaves, optional

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast into pieces. Make a marinade from 1 tsp. paprika powder, 1 tbsp. lime or lemon juice, 1 tsp. salt, 1 cup yogurt, 1 tsp. cayenne pepper, 1 tbsp. garam masala powder, 1 piece ginger and 1 clove garlic. Mix the meat with the marinade.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 °C top/bottom heat then cook the meat in a baking dish for 25 minutes.
  3. Finely chop the onion and sauté in 2 tablespoons of butter until translucent. Add the strained tomatoes, cinnamon, 1 tsp. salt, 2 tsp. cayenne pepper, 1 piece of ginger and 1 clove of garlic. Simmer everything for 20 minutes with the lid on and at a low temperature. Stir occasionally. Now add the remaining 2 tbsp butter, honey and cream, simmer for another 3 minutes. Remove the meat from the marinade, add to the sauce, stir briefly and simmer for 2 minutes.
